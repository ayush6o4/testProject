1.In this application the address of the nearest Police Station,Fire station, Hospital will be provided. 
2.So, mainly the whole application is be based on the 'Location of the user'. 
3.The location of the user will be shared with the emergency services and the emergency contacts by a single click on the screen or by pressing the power button thrice. 
4.Then the user will see a pop-up after opening the application or pressing the power button thrice stating "emergency department you are in need" giving them the options like a) Police , b) Fire, c) Medical. 
5.There will be Particular button for the particular type of emergency service that the user stands in need.
6.The main purpose of this application is to never make a person feel alone in an emergency situation.
